Briards

A HEART WRAPPED IN FUR

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog tradiyionally used as herder and guardian of sheep.

Breed History

The briard, which is believe to have orginated in France, has been bred for centuries to hard and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Amy as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded solidiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briards were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction.Currently the population of briards is slowly recovering.Charlemagne, Napoleon,Thomas,Jefferson and Lafayette all owned briards
